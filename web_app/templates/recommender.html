How does it work?

The recommender consists of 4 core recommenders utilizing different types of knowledge to come up with recommendations:

TF-IDF

Finds the most similar papers based on TF-IDF based similarity as calculated on abstract, title and journal.

Semantic Analysis

Finds the most semantically similar papers using manually annotated semantic tags of each paper.

Outgoing Citations

Finds paper-X such that papers cited by this paper are also cited by paper-X.

Incoming Citations

Finds paper-X such that papers who cited this paper also cited paper-X.

Aggregation Engine

All recommendations are processed by an aggregation algorithm that comes up with the optimal list of most relevant papers.
